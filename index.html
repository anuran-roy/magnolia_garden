Welcome to Magnolia Gardens

This land was purchased in and around 2018, with the plan to develop a project which would bring the villagers work and help them grow independently. When we bought this land, it was entirely a banana cultivation farm but gradually we tried converting and expanding the land for cultivation of exotic flora and fauna. At the garden there is a huge collection of mango plantations which are sourced from our neighboring countries like Bangladesh, Thailand and nevertheless from different parts of West Bengal. We also have organic farming which is based upon seasonal vegetables like brocolli, papaya, cauliflower, beans, carrots and many more. Above all we also present a wide variety of exotic fruits, viz. The world famous DRAGON fruit, Passion fruit, banana,etc. Apart from farming, we also intended to rent the land for PICNICS, DESTINATION WEDDING OR SHOOTING DOCUMENTARIES ON YOUTUBE. It's an earnest request to all of you reading this, please visit our garden, encourage us and spread your joy and experience to others.
